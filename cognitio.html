The participants will have to draw the simulation network from the topics they have studied in basic electrical engineering.

Microprocessor Coding

The participants will be asked to perform basic logical (merging, insertion, deletion etc.) and arithmetic operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication & division) of microprocessor 8085 & 8086.

Participants interested in drawing & poster making can take part in this competition.

Circuit designing involves the designing of basic and daily use circuits like extension board, circuit of tube light etc. Students are requested to study about basic circuits and if any doubt occurs the can contact at Basic Electrical Engineering Lab.

Project Contest

The project contest includes making any small project on any relevant ideas related to electrical engineering or any other topics.
